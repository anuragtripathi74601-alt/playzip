🎯 Snooker — Rules Guide

Goal: Doosre player se zyada points score karo! Pehle Red balls pot karo, phir Color balls pot karo.
🔴 Phase 1: Reds Phase (15 Red Balls)
Step 1: Pehle aap ek 🔴 Red ball pot karo (1 point)
Step 2: Phir koi bhi 🎨 Color ball pot karo — Yellow(2), Green(3), Brown(4), Blue(5), Pink(6), Black(7)
Step 3: Color pot karne ke baad wapas 🔴 Red pot karo
Repeat: Red → Color → Red → Color... jab tak saare 15 reds khatam na ho jayein!
💡 Note: Colors pot karne ke baad wapas apni jagah par aa jaate hain (re-spot)
🎨 Phase 2: Colors Phase (6 Color Balls)
Saare reds khatam hone ke baad, colors pot karo iss order mein:
1️⃣ Yellow (2 pts)
2️⃣ Green (3 pts)
3️⃣ Brown (4 pts)
4️⃣ Blue (5 pts)
5️⃣ Pink (6 pts)
6️⃣ Black (7 pts) — Last ball! 🏆
💡 Colors phase mein colors wapas nahi aate — permanently pot ho jaate hain!
🚫 Fouls & Penalties
• Wrong ball pot karna: Red ke bajay color, ya color ke bajay red pot kiya — 🚫 Foul!
• Foul penalty: Minimum 4 points, ya pot ki gayi ball ke points (jo bhi zyada ho) opponent ko jaate hain
• Cue ball pot: Foul — opponent ko ball-in-hand milta hai
• Colors phase mein galat color: Galat order mein color pot kiya — foul + penalty + color wapas aa jaata hai
• Agar aap foul karte hain, toh aapka turn khatam ho jaata hai
✅ Turn Continuation
• Agar aap sahi ball pot karte hain (red jab red chahiye, color jab color chahiye), toh aapka turn continue hota hai! 🔄
• Turn tab tak continue hota hai jab tak aap miss na karein, galat ball na pot karein, ya foul na karein
• Isiliye professional snooker mein 100+ points ka break possible hai!
💡 Tips & Strategy
• 🔴 Black ball (7 pts) pot karna sabse faydemand hai jab color ka time ho
• 🎯 Red pot karte waqt soch ke karein ki agli baar kaunsa color pot karna hai
• 🛡️ Cue ball ko aisi jagah rakhne ki koshish karein jahan se agla shot easy lage
• 📊 Scoreboard dekhte rahein — reds count, next ball, aur foul count track karein
