♟️ Chess — Rules Guide

🎯 Goal: Opponent ke King ko checkmate karo! Har player ke paas 16 pieces hote hain.
👑 Pieces & Unki Movements
♙♟ Pawn (8) — Sirf aage badhta hai. Pehli baar mein 2 square, uske baad 1 square. Tirchha (diagonal) pakadta hai. Promotion bhi kar sakta hai!
♖♜ Rook (2) — Seedha row aur column mein aage-piche, left-right. Unlimited squares!
♘♞ Knight (2) — L-shape mein chalta hai (2+1). Pieces ke upar se kood sakta hai!
♗♝ Bishop (2) — Sirf tirchha (diagonal) mein chalta hai. Apne color par hi rehta hai.
♕♛ Queen (1) — Sabse taakatwar piece! Seedha aur tirchha, dono taraf ja sakta hai.
♔♚ King (1) — Har taraf 1 square. Isko check mein bachana zaroori hai!
🎮 Game Flow — Step by Step
Step 1: ⚪ White (Aap) pehle move karte hain. Piece ko click karo, phir destination click karo
Step 2: ⚫ Black (AI) apna move karta hai. Wait karo — AI sochega
Step 3: Pieces ko pakdo — opponent ke piece ke square par jaakar capture karo
Step 4: 🌱 Pawn promotion: Agar pawn board ke aakhri tak pahunch gaya, toh usse Queen/Rook/Bishop/Knight banao
Step 5: 👑 Castling: King aur Rook ek saath move kar sakte hain (special move)
Step 6: 🏆 Checkmate: King ko bachane ki koi jagah nahi — YOU WIN!
Step 7: 🤝 Draw/Stalemate: Agar koi legal move nahi hai aur king check mein nahi hai — draw!
⚡ Special Moves
👑 Castling: King ko 2 square Rook ki taraf slide karo, Rook king ke paas aa jayega. King aur Rook dono first move nahi hone chahiye, aur beech mein koi piece nahi hona chahiye
🫴 En Passant: Agar opponent ka pawn 2 square move karta hai aur aapke pawn ke paas se guzarta hai, toh aap use capture kar sakte ho jaise woh 1 square move kiya ho
🌱 Promotion: Pawn jab board ke aakhri row par pahunchta hai, toh use Queen (♛), Rook (♜), Bishop (♝), ya Knight (♞) mein badal sakte hain
📝 Chess Notation (Move History Samajhna)
e4 = Pawn e4 par gaya (file e, rank 4)
Nf3 = Knight f3 par gaya
Bxe5 = Bishop ne e5 par capture kiya (x = capture)
O-O = Kingside castling
O-O-O = Queenside castling
e8=Q = Pawn e8 par pahuncha, Queen bana
+ = Check! ⚠️
# = Checkmate! 🏆

💡 Beginner Tips
• 🏰 Pehle center control karein — e4, d4 pawns aage badhayein
• 🦅 Pehle Knights aur Bishops develop karein, phir Queen
• 👑 Castle early — King ko safe rakhna zaroori hai!
• 🔗 Pieces ko ek saath kaam karne dein, akela piece mat bhejein
• 🧠 Soch-samajh ke khelein: Har move ke baad sochein ki opponent kya karega
• Undo button se apni galti wapas le sakte hain (practice ke liye)
